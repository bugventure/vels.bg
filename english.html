About Us

We are an accounting bureau with traditions in Plovdiv and the region.

With more than 20 years of experience, we offer high-quality accounting services at affordable fees. Our customers are small and medium companies from diverse branches: production, commerce, import, export, agriculture, food, restaurant, tourism, timber and construction industries. Although quite different in business operation, they all share one common trait - the specialized accounting services they get.

Professionalism is a priority for us, as well as our clients. We are on par with changes in legislation that affect business, as well as new tendencies and best practices.
